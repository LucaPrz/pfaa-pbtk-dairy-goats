3 Results

3.1 GOF

- For 15 of 30 compounds the model can reproduce the experimental data
- Main challenges are noisy and censored data
- Plasma, milk and solid tissues are well represented, feces and especially urine are more difficult to model

3.2 Kinetic characteristics in dairy goats

- steady state transferrates and time to reach the steady state
- systemic elimintaion half-lives
- correlation between transferrates and half lives -> PFAAs with longer half lives tranfer more to milk

3.3 Parameter identifiability (FIM)

- parameter correlations
- parameter gradients (sensitivity)

3.4 Application

- scenario analysis of different exposure over a full lactation cycle
- maximum allowable feed estimation to comply with EU indicative levels
